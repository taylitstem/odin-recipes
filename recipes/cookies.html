Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • granulated sugar
  • egg
  • maple syrup
  • pure vanilla extract
  • gluten free 1-to-1 flour (Bob's Red Mill is what I prefer)
  • sea salt
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • any chocolate chips you use (I love using semi-sweet dark chocolate)
  • flaky sea salt for that slighty salty topping texture

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven for 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar. Add the egg and mix again. Add honey and vanilla, and mix. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt, baking soda, and baking powder. Add this to the creamed butter mixture, and mix until combined.
  4. Fold the chocolate chips in. Cover the bowl and put it in the fridge to chill for at least 2 hours.
  5. Line a baking sheet or cookie sheet with parchment paper. Scoop out cookie dough onto parchment paper using a cookie scoop (mine holds 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon, or 20ml). Top with extra chocolate chips and sprinkle a pinch of flaky salt on each cookie. Bake for 8-9 minutes.
  6. Let them cool on the cookie sheet for a few minutes before moving them onto a wire rack to cool completely.
