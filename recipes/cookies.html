Gluten Free Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • granulated sugar
  • egg
  • maple syrup
  • pure vanilla extract
  • gluten free 1-to-1 flour (Bob's Red Mill is what I prefer)
  • sea salt
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • any chocolate chips you use (I love using semi-sweet dark chocolate)
  • flaky sea salt for that slighty salty topping texture
